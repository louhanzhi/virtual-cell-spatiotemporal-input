## In One Sentence
The paper introduces Deep Visual Proteomics (DVP), a workflow that couples AI-driven whole-slide image analysis and single-cell/single-nucleus laser microdissection (LMD) with ultra-high-sensitivity mass spectrometry, so that unbiased single-cell-type proteomes can be measured while their spatial and subcellular context is preserved — demonstrated on cultured cells (cell-cycle/nuclear heterogeneity) and on archived FFPE tumor tissue (salivary gland carcinoma; spatially resolved melanoma progression).

## Orientation (non-binding)
